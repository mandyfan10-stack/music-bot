## 2024-03-21 - Icon-Only Buttons and Form Inputs Missing Accessibility Labels
**Learning:** This application heavily utilizes Lucide icons for interactive elements (like clearing searches, closing modals, deleting reviews, and liking releases) without accompanying text labels. Additionally, it uses `placeholder` attributes on inputs as the sole method of describing the input, lacking explicit `<label>` tags or `aria-label` attributes. This creates a significant accessibility barrier for screen reader users who would only hear "button" or "edit text" without context.
**Action:** Always verify that icon-only interactive elements and form inputs (especially those styled without visible labels) have descriptive `aria-label` attributes applied to ensure proper accessibility context. Also ensure `<img />` tags have `alt` tags.
